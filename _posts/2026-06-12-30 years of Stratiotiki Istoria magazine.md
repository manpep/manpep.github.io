The latest issue of _Stratiotiki Istoria_ [Military History] magazine has just been published and includes an article of mine examining the influence of the New Military History movement on Greek historiography.

This year marks the magazine’s 30th anniversary. On a personal level, I am especially pleased to have been part of its journey since 2014, contributing a total of 36 articles over the years.

Writing for Stratiotiki Istoria has been both a dream fulfilled and an invaluable learning experience. It provided me with a platform to explore and express my research interests in a rigorous way, while helping me develop my research and writing skills from my undergraduate years through to the present day.

I am grateful to have contributed, in my own small way, to the history of a publication that has played such an important role in promoting military history in Greece.
